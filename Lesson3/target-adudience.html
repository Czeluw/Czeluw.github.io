Target audience

Our website aims to reach the homes in our community, and its members of all ages. Whether the forecast might help anyone plan a picnic at the park or get ready for daily travels to work or school, we have everythig you need to know to conquer your day!

Personas

This is Michael. Michael is a grown man who never completed his education. However, he is a hard-working American who loves his office job and is always looking to engage in new endeavors. Michael likes to spend time with his friends and does not have much family near him. Michael relies on the forecast to plan his outdoor projects and activities.

  • "Will it rain today? Nobody knows how to drive in the rain."
  • "if it rains all week, you must know that the roads are actually the slickest in the first half an hour."

This is Barney. He is a middle-aged single parent. He believes that friends must be there to witness what you do and make life legendary. During his free time, he follows new ideas to meet women in all kinds of scenarios. In addition, Barney is very close with his parents, who are advanced in age, and has a small daughter. Barney and his family have a need to know the weather forecast so they can decide how to better spend their time together.

  • "I hope it's sunny until Sunday because it feels like a great time for a weekend at Barney's"
  • "I'm sure glad raining season is over since Tedd lost the yellow umbrella."
  • "I heard that next week's storm will be the biggest yet to hit this city."
