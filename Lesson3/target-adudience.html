Target audience

Our website aims to reach the homes in our community, and it's members of all edges. Whether it may help to plan a picnic at the park or get ready for daily travels at work and school, we have everythig you need to know to conquer your day!

Personas

This is Michael. Michael is a grown man who never completed his education. However, he is a hard working American who loves his office job and is always looking to engage in new endeavors. Michael likes to spend time with his friends, but does not have much family near him. Michael relies on the forecast to plan his outdoor projects.

  • "Will it rain today? Nobody knows how to drive in the rain."
  • "It will be raining all week. You know, the roads are actually the slickest in the first half an hour."

This is Barney. He is a middle aged single parent. He believes that if your friends are not there to witness what you do, life is not legendary. During his free time, he follows new ideas to meet women in all kinds of scenarios. In addition, Barney is very close to his parents who are advanced in age, and has a small daughter. They all have a need to know the weather forecast so they can decide how to better spend their time together.

  • "I hope it's sunny today, it feels like it's time for a weekend at Barney's"
  • "I'm sure glad raining season is over because I lost my yellow umbrella."
  • "I heard that next week's storm will be the biggest yet to hit this city."
